Laboratories

Learning in the laboratories at JHPS Rampally is programmed to be an experience, its students will cherish. They are organized to provide reinforcement to a large number of curricular concepts to the children, through hands on and real time visual experiences. The labs are technology enabled for experiential learning in its true sense and also with the help of software, 3-D animation, video projections, etc.

The Laboratory Complex, in about 20,000 Sq Ft floor area, is almost the best as it comes and includes Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematic, Geography and Social Science Labs which not only cater to the Secondary classes but are also programmed to be extensively used for explaining the course content to the Primary and Middle School classes also. These subject based independent International Standard Labs are also equipped with required apparatus and appropriate tools for imparting practical and application based knowledge to the students.

A special 3 D printer lab is equipped with 2 very large sized 3 D printers and 4 standard sized printers, which cater to the requirement of the project activities of and by the students. Students may request for any design printing contemplated by them for their project activities, which will be provided based on availability of suitable software and /or design, on a first come first served basis.

An exclusive Ideation Room for generating group creative projects and other creative activities is also provided. Supporting this activity the school also has a Photography Hobby center clubbed with a journalism den.

Familiarizing students with the knowledge of where their food comes from and the various facets of farming practices is an added incentive for the children to explore their academic zeal out in the open at the 2 acre farm plots unit, where over 40 types of crops, vegetables, fruits, tubers, creepers, leafy vegetables are grown in small plots, every year.

Activity Rooms:

Activity rooms at JHPS – Rampally JHPS – Rampally include the various subject Labs, computer lab, 3-D Lab, the farm plots, music, art, dance, craft rooms, the huge auditorium, library, etc., which provide reinforcement to the areas of the learning processes of the students. Hands on activities are performed for the lessons learnt in different subjects. The entire campus is networked with INTRANET facility and 24/7 INTERNET connectivity for the benefit of students and staff in the instructional process.

An exclusive Kindergarten Activity center helps the Tiny-Tots enjoy their time with all their dream activities such as tree houses, play house, indoor lawns, etc.

Training is provided in the areas of Fine Arts, Dance, Music and Various Co-Scholastic Activities (CSA) and the various intra-disciplinary activities is organized by the regular teaching faculty in providing the additional curricular support needed for the comprehensive learning of the subject matter content through the medium of Dance, Music and Art within the various special activity rooms.
