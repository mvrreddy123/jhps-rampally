The Academic Year

The Academic Year usually starts on the 1st of April, each year. In the First session, classes are conducted for about 3 weeks subsequent to which the summer vacation is given for an about 6 weeks. School usually reopens during the 2nd Week of June.

The Annual Academic Session is divided into two terms (semesters).

  • The First Term - April to September
  • The Second Term - October to March
