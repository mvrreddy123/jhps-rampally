Vanamahotsavam

Jubilee Hills Public School, Rampally conducted a Special Assembly for Vanamahotsavam on 10th July. The Academic Coordinator, Jubilee Hills Public School, Ms. Sonia Nagpal and The Principal, Jubilee Hills Public School, Rampally Ms. Namrata Sandhu were present to grace the occasion. The programme began with a sloka invoking the blessings of the Lord. This was followed by a sequence of wonderful cultural events presented by the students of Jubilee Hills Public School, Rampally. The assembly consisted of a dance, song and a ballet stating the importance of both trees and forests. It also laid the importance of saving, protecting and planting more trees not only for the present generations but also for future generations. The Academic Coordinator, Jubilee Hills Public School, Ms. Sonia Nagpal and The Principal, Jubilee Hills Public School, Rampally Ms. Namrata Sandhu participated in the programme and along with the students of class VIII planted saplings in the school premises which initiated the Vanamahotsavam programme. With this the assembly came to an end and all the students dispersed to their classes Posted on July 10, 2019
